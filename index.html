About Company


We are a team of passionate software engineers, product managers, and designers that enjoy building
outstanding web and mobile applications.


At Nimble, we are proud to be an equal opportunity employer, committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive
workplace where all individuals are treated with respect and have an equal opportunity to thrive.



    What will we do?

  • You will be an active member or lead a team of developers in a project squad. We have a wide
    range of project sizes.
  • Decide which technologies are going to be used for front-end, back-end and define the overall architecture.
  • Work closely with the Product Owner to make sure the backlog is efficiently planned
  • Write clean, composable, and maintainable code as you develop world-class products for our clients.
  • Knowledge sharing through team rituals.


Why you'll love working here?

  • You will be proud of any product that has our name on it.
  • Great equipment consisting of a MacBook Pro, large screens, and any
    accessory to let you work comfortably. Our equipment is renewed every 3 years.
  • Our office kitchen is fully stocked with drinks and snacks for the team.


Qualification

  1. Professional web development with 3+ years of experience. Experience in Ruby,
    JavaScript or Go is ideal (at least one of them).
  2. Comfortable working both on frontend and backend.
  3. JavaScript framework like React JS/Redux, Vue JS or Angular JS.
